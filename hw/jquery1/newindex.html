5 easy steps for skin care

Cleansing one's skin is very important because it removes all of the dirt, sweat and oil that has been collected. In order to keep your pores clean one must clean their skin every morning and evening at least three to five times a week. One must purchase a cleanser based on skin type.

Toner helps remove any dirt uncessary residue that is left behind. One should tone right after cleaning by soaking a cotton ball or pad with the toner and apply by creating an upward movement starting at your neck.

Exfoliating will help remove dead skin cells which have been accumulated in one's pores. This helps with removing blackheads aswell. One should exfoliate at least three times a week.

Since exfoliating leaves one's skin bare it is important to create a level of protecting by adding moizturizer. This will hydrate the skin leaving it clean and smooth. One's mosterizer should vary based on skin type.
